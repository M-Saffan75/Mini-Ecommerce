Jeans are often considered a classic. Almost everyone from small children to adults own at least one pair of jeans. Men used jeans as their classic form for many years, while women often take the classic and put twists to update its look. Jeans used to be the most important apparel for every woman’s wardrobe.

Women’s Jeans: Currently, women’s jeans have more variety other than the super tight and high waisted jeans of the 80s. Those days are gone when women often wore too long blouses and shirts to hide the bulge caused by the unrelenting denim fabric. Baggy jeans are also considered fashion flubs. Jeans today are all about choice and more choice.
